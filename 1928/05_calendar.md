*Page xlvi*

*¶ The Letters denote the Days of the Week — A Sunday, b Monday, c Tuesday, d Wednesday, e Thursday, f Friday, g Saturday — when A is the Sunday Letter for the year. The Numbers prefixed to certain Days in March and April are the Golden Numbers for calculating Easter.*
