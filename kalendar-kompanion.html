Kalendar Kompanion

Any date. Every step shown.

This is not a search. Every result below is computed step by step directly from TABLE I (Dominical Letter) and TABLE II (Golden Number → Paschal Full Moon) as they appear in the 1928 Book of Common Prayer, Tables and Rules for the Movable and Immovable Feasts (pp. l–lii), transcribed in this repository. The same method a parish clerk used in 1662 — or 1928 — runs here, against the same tables. Each step shows its formula, its result, and its source so you can follow the logic yourself and verify it against the printed book. The goal is not just to give you the answer — it is to show you the assumptions behind it, so you understand what any calendar software is doing when it gives you the same answer automatically.

Select a date above and press Calculate.
