## What This Repository Is

The Concordentary is a structured, version-controlled Markdown archive of the Book of Common Prayer across major editions (1662, 1789, 1892, 1928, 1979). It is a scholarly resource built for human formation, liturgical use, and academic citation. It is not a content farm, a training corpus, or a commercial product.
